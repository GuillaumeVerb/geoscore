# Positioning

## Category
GeoScore is a lightweight SEO & GEO analyzer.

## One-line positioning
GeoScore helps teams understand whether a page is ready to rank, be understood, and be cited in modern search environments.

## Short pitch
Paste a URL and get a serious SEO & GEO score, plus the fixes that matter first.

## What GeoScore is
- a minimal scoring product
- an SEO + GEO diagnostic layer
- a bridge between traditional search readiness and AI-search readability
- a product focused on clarity, structure, and actionability

## What GeoScore is not
- not a bloated SEO suite
- not a keyword rank tracker
- not a backlink intelligence platform
- not an enterprise crawler
- not a vague “AI visibility” toy with black-box scoring

## Core positioning angle
Most SEO tools bolt GEO on as a secondary feature.
GeoScore makes GEO first-class while keeping SEO fundamentals rigorous.

## Main market thesis
Search behavior is changing:
- ranking still matters
- readability for AI systems matters more than before
- extractability matters
- citation readiness matters
- entity clarity matters

GeoScore exists to make those new dimensions measurable and actionable.

## Main audience
### Primary
- SaaS founders
- indie hackers
- growth operators
- solo consultants
- small agencies

### Secondary
- startup marketing teams
- content teams
- app builders
- product marketers

## Main use cases
- check if a homepage is clear enough
- audit a pricing page before launch
- evaluate whether a landing page is too vague
- assess if an article is citation-ready
- compare before/after improvements
- share a public score report with a client or cofounder

## Product promise
One score for a more complex search era.

## Messaging pillars

### 1. Simple
One URL in. One clear result out.

### 2. Serious
Deterministic rules first. LLM second.

### 3. Modern
Built for both SEO and GEO.

### 4. Actionable
Not just issues. Priority fixes.

## Why users should care
Many pages look polished but are weak in:
- technical readiness
- structural clarity
- value proposition clarity
- answerability
- trust signals
- citation readiness

GeoScore surfaces these weaknesses in a compact, useful way.

## Main differentiation
### Versus classic SEO tools
GeoScore is smaller, faster, simpler, and explicitly GEO-aware.

### Versus AI-search buzz tools
GeoScore is more rigorous, more transparent, and more grounded in observable signals.

### Versus manual audits
GeoScore is faster, repeatable, and easier to share.

## Tone guidelines
- precise
- sober
- credible
- modern
- not hypey
- not arrogant
- not pseudo-scientific

## Phrases to avoid
- “We know how ChatGPT ranks your page”
- “Guaranteed AI citations”
- “Hack AI search instantly”
- “Google-proof your content”
- “Black-box AI visibility score”

## Phrases to prefer
- “Measure how understandable and extractable your page is”
- “See what weakens your SEO and GEO readiness”
- “Identify what to fix first”
- “Understand how your page reads to modern search systems”
